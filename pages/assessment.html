What This Measures

It's Not Burnout. It's Misalignment.

Most high-capacity people aren't stuck because they lack strategy, discipline, or drive. You didn't arrive here because you failed. You arrived here because you succeeded — and the success feels heavier than it should.

This assessment measures the gap between who you've become and the role you're still playing. It's the invisible weight you carry every day. The exhaustion that sleep won't fix. The relationships that suffer even though you love them.

This is not a personality test. It won't type you or label you. It's a diagnostic mirror. It will show you exactly where the misalignment lives in your life.

After Your Results

What Your Score Means

Your results will show you one of three profiles. Each reveals something specific about where the misalignment is most pronounced in your life.

  • Early Tension (Score: 30-50). You're beginning to feel the gap between who you are and the role you're playing. The discomfort is noticeable but manageable. You're at a decision point — address it now, or wait until it gets louder.
  • Significant Misalignment (Score: 51-70). You're carrying real weight. The role is exhausting. You've been running on fumes longer than you'd like to admit. Your relationships and energy are showing the strain. Something has to change.
  • Critical Misalignment (Score: 71+). You're at the edge. The old identity is breaking down. This is either your breaking point or your breakthrough — but you can't stay where you are much longer.
